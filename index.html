About MindWeal

Untangle | Heal | Thrive

Our Story
MindWeal by Pihu Suri was founded with a vision to create a world where mental well-being is a priority, not an afterthought. In today's fast-paced and complex world, individuals often struggle to navigate their emotions, challenges, and inner conflicts. MindWeal was born out of a deep commitment to guiding people toward psychological resilience, healing, and self-discovery.

The name MindWeal reflects this mission:

  • Mind represents the thoughts, emotions, and experiences that shape our lives.
  • Weal—an ancient term for health and well-being—symbolizes the balance and fulfillment we strive to cultivate.

    • Together, MindWeal embodies the journey from mental struggle to emotional thriving. With a compassionate and evidence-based approach, we offer personalized therapy, group counseling, and community-driven initiatives designed to foster resilience, clarity, and personal growth.



    Message From The Founder


    “Mental well-being is not just about overcoming struggles—it’s about creating a life where you truly thrive. At MindWeal, we believe in providing a safe, inclusive, and nurturing space where individuals can explore their emotions without fear or judgment. Our goal is to help you untangle the complexities of your mind, heal from within, and step into a future filled with confidence and clarity."


    — Pihu Suri, Founder & Lead Psychologist

    Our Mission
